Infix functions get a first-class prefix alias in the language that can be used
in higher order functions

Special trick: these could be implemented in the language!

+     add
-     sub
*     mul
/     div
^     pow
and   _and      -- ugly
or    _or       -- ugly
=     eq
!=    neq
<     lt
<=    leq
>     gt
>=    geq


?     typeof
